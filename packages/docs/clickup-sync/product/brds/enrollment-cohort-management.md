Purpose/Problem: A published program with no way to admit and track learners isn't a product. Creators need to invite, approve, and organize learners into cohorts or self-paced tracks without manual spreadsheet work.

Business Objective: Let a creator move a learner from invited to active with minimal manual steps, supporting both scheduled cohorts and rolling self-paced enrollment.

Target Users: Creators (manage enrollment), learners (are enrolled).

Scope In: Enrollment states (pending\_approval, active, completed, dropped), approval-gated private programs, cohort scheduling.

Scope Out: Payments/paid-access gating (deferred), waitlisting beyond basic pending state.

Success Metrics: Time from invite to active enrollment; % pending approvals actioned within 48h; enrollment-to-completion rate by program shape.

Assumptions: Enrollment record resolves to phone number, not email (ADR-001).

Dependencies: Program & Curriculum Builder, Identity & Auth, Scheduling & Drip Engine.
