Purpose/Problem: Creators currently have no way to package their expertise into a structured, repeatable program. Without a builder, every cohort or learner relationship is bespoke and manual.

Business Objective: Let a creator go from idea to published program in one sitting, with no engineering or support involvement.

Target Users: Creators / mentors / trainers.

Scope In: Program creation (shape + visibility), Module to Lesson structure, lesson types (text/video-embed/attachment/live/in-person), draft/publish states.

Scope Out: Quiz lesson type, AI scaffolding, hosted video upload.

Success Metrics: % creators publishing within 24h; % programs reaching an active enrollment within 14 days.

Dependencies: Multi-tenancy, Identity and Auth, Design System.
